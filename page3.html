Boolean battle

Boolean Battle is a group event with puzzles, debugging, and designing tasks on digital circuits like logic gates, number systems, and combinational/ equential circuits. Teams will tackle challenges to enhance their circuit analysis and design skills. The event fosters technical growth in a competitive setting.
